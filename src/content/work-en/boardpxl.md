## About the project

**SportPXL Dashboard** is a full-stack web application built to centralise financial flow management for the [**SportPXL**](https://sportpxl.com/) platform. It gives photographers and administrators a dedicated space to track transactions, manage credits, and review their financial history clearly and efficiently.

## Key features

- Centralised invoice and transaction management
- Real-time photographer credit tracking
- Integration with **Stripe**, **Pennylane**, and **ForestAdmin**
- Intuitive dashboard adapted to different roles (photographer / administrator)
- Complete financial history
